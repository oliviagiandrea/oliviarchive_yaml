---
title: "Baked Tortilla Eggs"
date: "2024-02-04"
servings: 1
minutes: 45
categories: ['single serve', 'vegetarian', 'mains']
notes: "Careful not to overcook - the yolks are best slightly runny."
ingredientsList: ['tortillas', 'eggs', 'black beans', 'salsa', 'feta cheese']
ingredients: "<li>1 tortilla</li><li>2 eggs</li><li>90g black beans</li><li>30g chunky salsa</li><li>15g feta cheese</li><li>70g honeydew</li>"
directions: "<li>Preheat oven to 350F.</li><li>Put your tortilla in a 6-inch circular cake pan. Crack your eggs on top of it, then top with vegetables, beans, and cheese.</li><li>Bake for 25 minutes or until whites of eggs are set. Serve with honeydew on the side.</li>"
