date: '2024-02-04'
servings: 24
minutes: 60
notes: ""
categories: ['desserts', 'cookies']
ingredientsList: ['butter', 'powdered sugar', 'salt', 'all-purpose flour', 'cornflour', 'tapioca flour', 'baking powder', 'egg yolk', 'egg']
ingredients: "<li>1 cup (2 sticks) unsalted butter, softened</li><li>1/2 cup powdered sugar</li><li>1/2 tsp salt</li><li>1 1/2 cups all-purpose flour</li><li>2 tbsp cornflour</li><li>3/4 cup tapioca flour</li><li>1/2 tsp baking powder</li><li>1 egg yolk, lightly beaten</li>"
directions: "<li>Preheat oven to 300F.</li><li>In an electric mixer, cream together butter, powdered sugar, and salt until light and fluffy.</li><li>Sift in all three flours and baking powder. Mix again until fully combined.</li><li>Drop by tsp scoop onto greased baking sheets. Brush each cookie with the egg yolk to glaze them, then bake for 20 minutes or until the cookies are lightly golden-brown on the bottoms.</li><li>Let cool on the baking sheet for 5 minutes, then finish cooling entirely on wire racks before serving.</li>"
